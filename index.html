About

What I do and why it matters.

I'm Zyad, a developer who cares about building things that work well and last. I've founded Infiniware to focus on creating tools and systems that solve real problems.

Every project I work on reflects my philosophy: clear architecture, attention to detail, and zero bloat. I code in Python, JavaScript, C++, and more. I contribute to open source. And I'm always learning from others' work.

Approach

Building things the right way.

I believe in simplicity. Every line of code, every design decision, every feature— it all needs a reason to exist. If it doesn't pull its weight, it goes away.

I care about making code that's readable, maintainable, and honest. I care about performance, security, and user experience. But more than anything, I care about building things I'm proud to have my name on.

Current focus and ongoing projects.
